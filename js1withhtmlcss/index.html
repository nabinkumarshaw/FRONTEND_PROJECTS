spider man

About

Spider-manis a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.created by writer-editor Stan Leeand artist Steve Ditko,he first appeared in the anthology comic books Amazing Fantasy #15(August 1962)in the Silver Age of Comic Books.He has been featured in comic books,television shows,films,video games,novels,and plays
